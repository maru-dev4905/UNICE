About UNICE

Everyone has a chance to
be healty. It is realized with
blockchain, metaverse,
and digital healthcare.

UNICE, which has been working on international medical service for more
than 30 years, allows everyone to take care of their health using blockchain,
metaverse and digital healthcare. The cooperation between medical
personnel and experts in each field is added to UNICE’s platform, which
provides horizontal health networks to the world.

About UNICE

Data openness

An agreement system for defining and managing health information is a major
component of UNICE. We provide a platform with public interest value so that
everyone, including professional medical personnel, as well as users
who are interested in their own health information, can freely use data.

Personalized Health Care

UNICE realizes health care for ‘me’ beyond user-centeredness. It provides everyone
with open technologies and services to pursue happiness and health and guarantees
the authority to access information and services for you to live a health life.

Promotion of Health Activities

UNICE’s goal is to synchronize all of the user’s health activities and the virtual world.
The user experience in the virtual world can give a deeper understanding of health care
and promote to perform easily activities for health care. The more users participate in
health activities, the more UNICE rewards and benefits are given.

Major Services

Introducing the advanced
services of UNICE.

Medical service

We will continue to provide medical services for medically
underprivileged areas base on our long-term medical
service experience. We will show innovation in medical
service through blockchain telemedicine, artificial intelligence
diagnosis, and global medical service standardization.

Telemedicine

It is a medical platform service that allows people to use
advanced medical services and health care regardless of
region or distance through non-face-to-face medical services.

My Care Bot

It is a virtual caregiver who manages your health information
and provides a guide to help you become healthier through
artificial intelligence.

Health Avatar

You can enjoy rewards and healthy life by exercising with
metaverse avatar which reflects your activities for health
and managing your diet.
